Employee Remote Work Policy

Employees are permitted to work remotely up to 3 days per week, subject
to manager approval. Fully remote arrangements require VP-level sign-off
and are reviewed quarterly.

All remote employees must be reachable during core hours, defined as
10:00 AM to 4:00 PM in their local time zone. Team meetings should be
scheduled within this window whenever possible.

Company equipment (laptop, monitor, headset) is provided for remote
work and must be returned upon termination of employment. Employees are
responsible for maintaining a secure home network and must use the
company VPN when accessing internal systems.

Reimbursement for home office setup is capped at $500 per employee per
calendar year and requires pre-approval from the employee's manager.

Employees relocating to a different state or country must notify HR at
least 30 days in advance, as this may affect payroll, tax withholding,
and benefits eligibility.

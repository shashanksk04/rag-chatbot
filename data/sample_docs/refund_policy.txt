Company Refund Policy

Customers may request a refund within 30 days of purchase, provided the
product is unused and in its original packaging. Refunds are processed
within 5-7 business days to the original payment method.

Digital products and software licenses are non-refundable once the
license key has been activated, except where required by local law.

For subscription services, customers can cancel at any time. Cancelling
a subscription stops future billing but does not refund the current
billing period unless the cancellation occurs within 48 hours of the
charge.

Damaged or defective items are eligible for a full refund or free
replacement, regardless of the 30-day window, provided the customer
reports the issue within 14 days of delivery and provides photo evidence.

To initiate a refund, customers should contact support@example.com with
their order number and reason for the request. Refund requests are
typically reviewed within 2 business days.
